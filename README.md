# 📚 llm_wiki - Turn documents into organized knowledge bases

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_for_Windows-blue)](https://github.com/Earthlike-hopper1684/llm_wiki)

LLM Wiki organizes your documents into a searchable, interlinked knowledge base. It scans your files and builds a persistent wiki that grows with your information. You no longer need to search through hundreds of files to find one specific fact. The application links related topics and creates a map of your data.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up the application on your Windows computer.

1. Go to the [official release page](https://github.com/Earthlike-hopper1684/llm_wiki).
2. Click the link to download the installer for Windows.
3. Open the downloaded file to start the setup process.
4. Follow the instructions on your screen.
5. Open the llm_wiki icon on your desktop.

## 🖥️ System Requirements

Ensure your computer meets these requirements for smooth performance:

*   Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   Processor: A modern multi-core processor.
*   Memory: 8 GB of RAM or more.
*   Storage: 2 GB of free space for the application and database files.
*   Graphics: A graphics card that supports hardware acceleration.

## 🛠️ How it Works

The application processes your documents in the background. It reads your text files, PDFs, and notes to identify key concepts. Instead of a traditional search, it links these concepts together. This creates a web of information. When you add a new document, the app updates the wiki. It saves your progress so you can pick up where you left off.

## 📁 Managing Your Data

You control which folders the application monitors. Open the settings menu to add or remove source folders. You can point the app to specific folders on your hard drive. 

*   Select the "Add Folder" button.
*   Browse to the folder that contains your documents.
*   Confirm the selection.

The application will then index the contents. Large numbers of files may take a few minutes to process during the first run. The status bar at the bottom of the window shows the progress.

## 🔍 Searching Your Knowledge Base

Search for any topic in the bar at the top of the window. The application shows results based on the connections between your documents. You can click on any result to see the original source or view related links. This view helps you discover connections between files you might have forgotten.

## ⚙️ Settings and Customization

Adjust the application settings to match your needs:

*   Display Mode: Switch between light and dark themes.
*   Auto-Update: Enable or disable automatic updates to the knowledge base when files change.
*   Language: Choose the primary language for your documents.

## 🔒 Privacy and Security

Your data stays on your computer. The application does not upload your documents to the cloud or share them with third parties. All indexing occurs locally. This ensures your private information remains private. 

## ❓ Troubleshooting

If the application fails to open, check the following:

*   Restart your computer and try again.
*   Verify you have sufficient disk space.
*   Ensure your antivirus software does not block the application. You may need to add an exception for the installation folder.
*   Reinstall the application using the latest version from the [download page](https://github.com/Earthlike-hopper1684/llm_wiki).

## 📋 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does this app store my files online?**
No. All files remain on your local machine.

**Can I export my knowledge base?**
You can export your database to a standard format through the File menu. This allows you to back up your data or move it to another machine.

**Why does the system use a lot of memory?**
The application uses memory to map the connections between documents efficiently. This improves the speed of your searches.

**Is my data safe during an update?**
Yes. The update process preserves your index and your settings.
